# Sneakers Streetwear Analysis

This dashboard was created in Excel and provides a clear overview of sneakers and streetwear sales across brands, product categories, customer segments, and regions. It showcases key metrics like total revenue, units sold, and monthly performance trends, helping to reveal customer behavior and overall sales performance.
The dashboard reveals a strong overall performance with total revenue of $176,353 generated from 1,097 units, indicating a healthy average unit price and steady transaction volume.

### Insights from the analysis includes:
- 🟠 Women contribute the highest share of revenue, followed by men and unisex, highlighting a stronger demand within the female segment. In terms of product categories, Casual wear leads significantly, with Streetwear and Limited Edition products also performing well, while Sportswear lags behind.
  
- 🟠 Brand performance shows Nike as the market leader, followed by Adidas and Off-White. On a geographical level, the USA generates the highest revenue, with the UK and Japan also contributing strongly, while countries like Germany and India show relatively lower performance.
  
- 🟠 The month-on-month analysis reflects fluctuations in revenue, with notable growth in certain months but also sharp declines in others, suggesting inconsistent sales performance that may be influenced by seasonality, promotions, or market demand shifts.

Overall, the business is driven by strong brand influence, higher engagement from female customers, and dominance in casual and streetwear categories, but faces challenges in maintaining consistent monthly growth across all regions.
